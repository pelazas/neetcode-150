BEST TIME TO BUY AND SELL STOCK - DIFFICULTY EASY

You are given an array `prices` where `prices[i]` is the price of a given stock on the `i`-th day.

GREEDY ALGORITHM WITH TWO pointers

HOW TO IDENTIFY THE ALGORITHM
- You are looking for an optimal solution by making a series of choices.
- You can solve the problem by choosing the best option at each step without worrying about the global

KEY TAKEAWAYS
- Use two pointers to track the minimum price and the current price.
- Update the maximum profit whenever a higher profit is found.
- Time complexity: O(n)
- Space complexity: O(1)
